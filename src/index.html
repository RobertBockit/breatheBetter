HERE'S HOW TO START WITH BREATHING EXERCISES

You don’t need BreatheBetter to practice breathing techniques, but it makes the process effortless, structured, and more effective! Whether you want to reduce stress, improve focus, or enhance overall well-being, mastering proper breathing can be a game-changer. Let’s walk through the simple steps to get started.

STEP 1

Select your favorite breathing exercise from a variety of scientifically backed techniques, such as Box Breathing for relaxation, 4-7-8 Breathing for better sleep, or Diaphragmatic Breathing for improved lung function. Find the one that best suits your needs and get ready to transform your breathing habits.

STEP 2

Try your chosen deep breathing exercise for the first time. Focus on slow, intentional breaths, inhaling deeply through your nose and exhaling completely through your mouth. Pay attention to how your body feels—calmer, more centered, and energized. Consistency is key, so take a few moments to practice daily.

STEP 3

Implement deep breathing into your daily routine for long-term benefits. Whether you integrate it into your morning meditation, use it to unwind before bed, or as a quick stress reliever during the day, making it a habit will help improve your mental clarity and physical health. BreatheBetter can support you by sending reminders and guided sessions to keep you on track!
